About Me

I was born and raised in Sacramento, California. My childhood was filled with fun and adventure having 5 other siblings to keep me entertained. In the Summer of 2012 my parents decided to move to the heat of Arizona. Here I was able to finish high school and then get my associates at Mesa Community College.

After switching degrees several time I struggled to find where my passions lie. Summer off 2017 I came across a coding bootcamp that allowed me to master the basics of Web Development. Having enrolled in the U of A Bootcamp program has allowed me to expand my knowledge of the ins and outs of websites as well as potential career options.
